Welcome to the Pomodoro Technique!

History

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length and separated by short breaks. Each interval is known as a pomodoro, from the Italian word for 'tomato'!

It’s as easy as 1, 2, 3!

Step 1

Take some time to think about the time each of your tasks are going to require. Add your tasks with the appropriate times to the tasks list and we’ll break down your work sessions into the appropriate pomodoro intervals!

Step 2

Press "Start"! You’ll be directed to the timer where you will alternate between work sessions, short breaks, and long breaks. Here is your chance to be productive with our application!

Step 3

After you’re done, we will provide an analysis of your work session!
